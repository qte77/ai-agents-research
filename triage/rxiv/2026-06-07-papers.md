### Physics Is All You Need? A Case Study in Physicist-Supervised AI Development of Scientific Software

- arxiv: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30353>
- summary: This study investigates the role of AI agents in research through a case study of a physicist supervising an AI coding agent to build a physics module, revealing critical insights into supervision practices and model limitations.
- methods: quantified case study, supervision events classification, oracle tests, parameter testing, changelog analysis
- findings: The AI agent resolved ten issues autonomously but struggled with root-cause resolution.; Supervision practices were critical for identifying issues that oracle tests missed.; The agent's inability to propose architectural alternatives limited its effectiveness.

### DynaFLIP: Rethinking Robotics Perception via Tri-Modal-Dynamics Guided Representation

- arxiv: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30350>
- summary: DynaFLIP is a dynamics-aware multimodal pre-training framework that enhances robot manipulation by integrating motion understanding into perception through image-language-3D flow triplets.
- methods: simplex-volume minimization, cosine regularizer, contrastive objective
- findings: DynaFLIP focuses on control-relevant regions critical for manipulation.; The resulting dynamics-aware representations serve as reusable visual backbones.; DynaFLIP consistently outperforms baselines across diverse downstream policies.; Gains reach +22.5% under out-of-distribution scenarios.
